About Rotaract 3080 • district identity and reach

We empower youth to develop leadership skills, foster responsible citizenship, and engage in meaningful service initiatives.

Rotaract District 3080 was established in RIY 1984-85 and spans Chandigarh (UT), Punjab, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, and parts of Uttar Pradesh. The district is built around clubs that combine service with leadership development and community engagement.

60+ clubs 6 zones Home district of Rtn. Rajendra K. Saboo
District story

Rotaract District 3080 in context

As part of the wider Rotary family, the district grows leaders through service, fellowship, and practical opportunities. The structure supports clubs across North India, enabling both local identity and district-wide collaboration.
