Rotary International • the wider movement

Rotary International gives the district its global language of service.

Rotary is a worldwide network of 45,000+ clubs and more than 1.4 million members united by service, fellowship, integrity, diversity, leadership, and the desire to create lasting change.

“Service Above Self” and the search for lasting change are not just ideas here; they define the whole system of action.
